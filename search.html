Delhi to Mumbai Journey Options

Compare travel options and their environmental impact

Train Journey

Mumbai to Delhi by Rail

Distance:1,384 km
Duration:15-18 hours
Est. Cost:₹1,500-₹5,000

Eco-Friendliness

Trains produce only 14g CO2 per passenger km - the most eco-friendly option.

  • Electric trains reduce carbon footprint
  • High passenger capacity maximizes efficiency
  • Lower emissions than road or air travel

Car Journey

Mumbai to Delhi by Road

Distance:1,414 km
Duration:20-24 hours
Est. Cost:₹8,000-₹12,000

Eco-Friendliness

Cars produce about 120g CO2 per passenger km - better with multiple passengers.

  • Emissions vary with car type and occupancy
  • Electric vehicles significantly reduce impact
  • Better than flying but worse than train/bus

Bus Journey

Mumbai to Delhi by Bus

Distance:1,414 km
Duration:20-22 hours
Est. Cost:₹1,200-₹2,500

Eco-Friendliness

Buses produce about 30g CO2 per passenger km - very efficient for group travel.

  • High passenger capacity reduces per-person emissions
  • Modern buses have better fuel efficiency
  • Good eco-friendly alternative to private cars

Flight Journey

Mumbai to Delhi by Air

Distance:1,130 km
Duration:2 hours
Est. Cost:₹3,000-₹10,000

Eco-Friendliness

Flights produce about 180g CO2 per passenger km - highest environmental impact.

  • High altitude emissions have greater warming effect
  • Non-CO2 emissions contribute significantly to climate impact
  • Short flights are particularly inefficient per km

Travel Responsibility Tips

  • Choose trains or buses for shorter domestic routes when possible
  • If flying is necessary, consider carbon offset programs
  • Carpooling significantly reduces per-person emissions for road travel
  • Off-peak travel can reduce congestion and improve efficiency
