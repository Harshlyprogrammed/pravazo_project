About Maharashtra

Maharashtra is India's second-most populous state and third-largest by area. Stretching from the coast of the Arabian Sea to the interior Deccan Plateau, it features diverse landscapes from bustling cities to serene hill stations, ancient caves to modern infrastructure.

The state is a cultural powerhouse, known for its Marathi heritage, Bollywood film industry, vibrant festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi, and delectable cuisine ranging from street food vada pav to sophisticated Kolhapuri dishes.

With a history dating back to ancient times, Maharashtra offers visitors a unique blend of historical monuments, natural beauty, and contemporary urban experiences.
